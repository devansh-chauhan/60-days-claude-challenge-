# Day 17 – Vehicle Fuel Analytics Dashboard using Claude

## Overview

On Day 17 of the **60 Days of Claude Challenge**, I used Claude to analyze a vehicle fuel dataset and generate a fully interactive fuel analytics dashboard from a CSV file.

The goal was to transform raw vehicle and fuel consumption data into meaningful insights through calculations, visualizations, and comparative analysis.

---

## Vehicle Configuration Used

| Parameter        | Value                |
| ---------------- | -------------------- |
| Vehicle          | Maruti Suzuki Ertiga |
| Fuel Type        | CNG                  |
| Usage Pattern    | Mixed                |
| Monthly Distance | 1200 km              |
| Vehicle Age      | 4 Years              |

---

## Dataset Analysis

Claude processed the CSV dataset and calculated:

### Fuel Performance Metrics

* Average Cost per KM
* Average CO₂ Emission per KM
* Average Maintenance Cost per KM
* Average Refuel / Recharge Time

### Age-Based Vehicle Analysis

Vehicles were categorized into:

* New (0–2 Years)
* Mid-Life (3–5 Years)
* Aged (6–9 Years)
* Old (10+ Years)

The selected vehicle age was highlighted across the dashboard visualizations.

---

## Advanced Fuel Analysis

### E85 Paradox Analysis

Calculated:

* Pump Price Saving
* Running Cost Penalty
* Break-Even Fuel Price

### E85 Performance Score

Scoring Criteria:

* Cost Efficiency
* CO₂ Emissions
* Refuel Time
* Maintenance Cost

**Final E85 Score:** 6.2 / 10

## Dashboard Features

### KPI Cards

---

## Key Findings

### CNG

✅ Lowest running cost among conventional fuels
✅ Suitable for high monthly usage
❌ Requires dedicated CNG infrastructure

### Electric Vehicle (EV)

✅ Lowest operating cost overall
✅ Lowest emissions
❌ Longer charging times

### E85 Fuel

✅ Lower emissions than Petrol and Diesel
✅ Eco-friendly alternative fuel
❌ Mileage penalty increases running cost

### Diesel

✅ Strong highway performance
✅ Better torque for long-distance driving
❌ Higher maintenance and emissions
