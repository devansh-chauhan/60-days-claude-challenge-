# Day 16 — Building a Reusable Stock Fundamental Research Skill in Claude

## Challenge Objective

Today's challenge focused on creating a **Custom Claude Skill** that can perform structured stock fundamental analysis without requiring the user to repeatedly paste long prompts.

The goal was to transform a detailed stock research prompt into a reusable AI skill capable of analyzing Indian and global listed companies using financial fundamentals, valuation metrics, business quality indicators, ownership patterns, and risk factors.

---

## Custom Skill Created

### Skill Name

**stock-fundamental-research**

### Description

Analyze Indian and global listed companies using fundamentals, financial statements, business quality, competitive advantages, valuation, risks, and growth prospects. Generate evidence-based research reports and investor-friendly summaries without providing direct investment advice.

---

## What the Skill Does

The skill can generate:

* Quick Stock Analysis
* Deep Fundamental Research Reports
* Company Comparisons
* Pros & Cons Analysis
* Portfolio Fit Analysis

It automatically evaluates:

* Valuation Metrics
* Financial Health
* Revenue Growth
* Profitability Trends
* Ownership Patterns
* Competitive Advantages
* Sector Tailwinds & Headwinds
* Business Risks

## Key Learnings

### 1. Custom Skills Save Time

Instead of repeatedly entering large prompts, the skill can be reused across multiple stock analyses.

### 2. Consistent Output Structure

Every report follows the same framework, making comparisons easier and more objective.

### 3. Prompt Engineering at Scale

Custom Skills convert complex prompts into reusable AI workflows.

### 4. Better Research Process

The skill ensures that important financial metrics are not overlooked during analysis.

### 5. AI as a Research Assistant

Claude can organize large amounts of financial information into structured reports, helping users perform systematic research.
