# Day 29 – Operation Lifeline: Supply Chain Crisis Lab

## Overview

**Operation Lifeline: Supply Chain Crisis Lab** is an interactive, single-file React-based simulation built as part of **Day 29 of the 60 Days of Claude Challenge**.

The application places users in the role of a Supply Chain Executive responsible for guiding a fictional company through unexpected supply chain disruptions. Players make strategic decisions involving crisis response, supplier negotiations, executive leadership, and AI investments while monitoring key business metrics in real time.

The simulator combines business strategy, operations management, and decision-making into an engaging educational experience designed for beginners.

---

## Features

### Simulation Flow

* Welcome screen with simulation overview
* Random fictional company generation
* Dynamic supply chain crisis scenarios
* Interactive War Room decision-making
* Supplier negotiation simulation (4 rounds)
* CEO Boardroom leadership assessment
* AI investment strategy selection
* Executive performance dashboard
* Replay with randomized scenarios

---

## Randomized Company Profile

Every simulation generates a unique organization including:

* Industry
* Annual Revenue
* Manufacturing Facilities
* Warehouses
* Supplier Network
* Inventory Coverage
* Lead Time
* Countries of Operation

---

## Crisis Scenarios

Possible disruptions include:

* Factory Fire
* Supplier Bankruptcy
* Port Strike
* Cyberattack
* Flood
* Raw Material Shortage
* Political Conflict
* Shipping Delay

Each crisis includes urgency level and estimated business impact.

---

## War Room

Players evaluate six response strategies and choose three.

Business metrics update dynamically:

* Cost
* Inventory
* Profit
* Delivery Speed
* Customer Satisfaction

Animated progress bars visualize the impact of each decision.

---

## Supplier Negotiation

Complete four negotiation rounds where each choice influences:

* Supplier Trust
* Pricing
* Lead Time

A final negotiation score measures supplier relationship effectiveness.

---

## CEO Boardroom

Answer five executive leadership questions focused on:

* Crisis Management
* Business Strategy
* Operational Leadership
* Customer Experience
* Long-Term Resilience

---

## AI Strategy

Choose two strategic AI investments from:

* Demand Forecasting
* Inventory Optimization
* Supplier Risk Monitoring
* Warehouse Vision
* Procurement Copilot

The simulator estimates operational improvements resulting from each investment.

---

## Executive Dashboard

At the end of each simulation, review:

* Overall Crisis Score
* Leadership Score
* Negotiation Score
* Business Resilience
* Cost Control
* Risk Management
* Customer Satisfaction

The dashboard also provides:

* Best Decision
* Biggest Mistake
* Personalized Feedback
* Expert Recommendation
* Lessons Learned
