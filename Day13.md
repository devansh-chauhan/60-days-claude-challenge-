# Day 13 – AI-Powered Job Discovery & Market Analysis with Claude Connectors

## Objective

The goal of Day 13 was to learn how Claude Connectors can be used for real-world job discovery and career planning.

Using the Indeed Connector, Claude analyzed my professional profile, searched for matching internship opportunities, evaluated job-fit scores, identified skill gaps, and provided market demand insights.

---

## Professional Profile Used

* Current Status: B.Tech CSE (AI/ML), Pre-Final Year Student
* Location: Kanpur, India
* Skills: Python, C, SQL (Basics), Data Analysis Fundamentals
* Certifications:

  * Python (HackerRank)
  * Python Essentials (Cisco)
  * Cybersecurity Certification
* CGPA: 7.3/10

## Biggest Learning

Finding opportunities is only one part of the process.

AI-powered job discovery can also help identify:

* Skill gaps
* Market demand trends
* Career readiness
* Best-fit roles

This makes job searching more strategic and data-driven rather than relying on random applications.
