#  Cognitive Pattern Explorer

An interactive, psychology-inspired self-reflection web application that helps users explore their thinking patterns through engaging scenarios and interactive activities. Designed as an educational experience, the application encourages reflection and self-awareness without making any clinical assessments.

##  Overview

**Cognitive Pattern Explorer** provides a calm, game-like environment where users navigate everyday situations, organize priorities, and map their decision-making process to better understand their cognitive tendencies.

The application emphasizes personal reflection using supportive, non-diagnostic language while offering meaningful insights into different thinking styles.

---

##  Features

-  Calm Mode and Stress Mode experiences
-  Interactive scenario-based self-reflection
-  Drag-and-drop priority exercises
-  Timeline-based thinking sequence activities
-  Personalized Thinking Profile
-  Reflection Journal with tailored insights
-  Percentage breakdown of thinking tendencies
-  Progress tracking across all chapters
-  Modern responsive interface
-  Keyboard accessibility and reduced motion support
-  Replay with different modes for comparison
-  Fully offline single-file HTML application

---

##  Application Flow

### 1. Start Screen
Choose between:
- Calm Mode
- Stress Mode

### 2. Chapter 1 – Discover Your Thinking Style
Respond to realistic everyday scenarios and explore how your choices reflect different thinking approaches.

### 3. Chapter 2 – Choose Your Priorities
Use drag-and-drop interactions to organize priorities and understand your natural decision-making preferences.

### 4. Chapter 3 – Map Your Thinking
Arrange your thinking process using interactive timelines to visualize how you approach problems.

### 5. Reflection Journal
Receive a personalized summary including:
- Thinking tendency percentages
- Reflection notes
- Comparative insights
- Suggestions for balanced decision-making

---

##  Thinking Tendencies Explored

- Analytical Thinker
- Emotional Intuitive
- Overthinking Loop Style
- Action-First Decision Maker
- Balanced Reflective Thinker

> **Educational Note:** This application is designed for learning and self-reflection only. It does not diagnose, assess, or evaluate mental health conditions.

---

##  Key Learning Outcomes

Through this experience, users learn to:

- Reflect on their decision-making process
- Recognize different thinking tendencies
- Understand how context influences choices
- Explore priorities during calm and stressful situations
- Develop greater self-awareness through guided reflection

---

##  Key Learnings

- Everyone approaches decisions differently depending on context.
- Reflection encourages better understanding rather than judgment.
- Stress and calm environments can influence thinking patterns.
- Self-awareness is a valuable skill for personal and professional growth.
- Interactive learning makes complex psychological concepts more accessible.
