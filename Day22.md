# Day 22 — Startup Validation Report

## Overview

Day 22 focused on validating a startup idea using AI-powered market research, founder-market fit analysis, and venture-style evaluation frameworks.

Since I did not have a startup idea initially, Claude guided me through a structured startup discovery process based on my background, interests, skills, and target market.

After evaluating multiple opportunities, I selected an **AI-Powered Placement & Interview Preparation Platform for Students** as the startup concept for validation.

---

## Startup Idea

### AI Placement & Career Preparation Platform

A platform designed to help college students prepare for placements through:

* AI-powered mock interviews
* Personalized learning roadmaps
* Resume analysis
* ATS optimization
* Skill-gap analysis
* Company-specific interview preparation
* Placement tracking and insights

### Target Market

* Engineering Students
* Final Year Students
* Placement Aspirants
* Tier 2 & Tier 3 College Students
* Fresh Graduates

Primary Market: India 🇮🇳
