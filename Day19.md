Day 19 – Football Intelligence Hub ⚽

Overview

For Day 19 of the 60 Days of Claude Challenge, I explored how Claude can combine sports analytics, knowledge assessment, and personality profiling into a single interactive experience.

Using a football workbook containing historical FIFA World Cup data, contender statistics, player performance metrics, and tournament insights, I generated a complete Football Intelligence Profile.

---

Objective

Build an AI-powered Football Intelligence Hub capable of:

- Predicting FIFA World Cup 2026 outcomes
- Assessing football knowledge through an interactive quiz
- Analyzing personality traits through a Messi vs Ronaldo comparison
- Generating a personalized Football Intelligence Profile
