#  Day 51 – Product Discovery & Sprint Planning

##  Overview

Day 51 marks the beginning of the **10-Day Capstone Project** in the **AB Talks 60-Day Claude AI Challenge**. Instead of directly building an application, this phase focuses on discovering the right product idea, validating it, defining a realistic MVP, and creating a detailed roadmap for successful implementation.

Working with **Claude AI** as a product mentor and technical advisor, I transformed an initial concept into a structured product plan supported by professional documentation.

---

##  Features

-  AI-guided product discovery
-  MVP scope definition
-  Feature prioritization
-  Professional Product Requirements Document (PRD)
-  Implementation Blueprint for Days 2–10
-  Project Pitch Deck
-  Scope management and exclusion planning
-  Success criteria definition
-  Software Development Lifecycle (SDLC) planning

---

##  Learning Outcomes

Through this project, I learned:

- Product Discovery Process
- MVP Planning
- Sprint Planning
- Product Requirement Documentation
- Scope Management
- AI-assisted Product Ideation
- Software Development Lifecycle (SDLC)
- Project Roadmapping


---

##  Key Takeaways

- Successful software starts with solving the right problem.
- A clear MVP is more valuable than an oversized feature list.
- Proper planning reduces development complexity.
- Professional documentation simplifies implementation.
- AI can effectively support product ideation and planning.
