#  Supply Chain Builder

An interactive, beginner-friendly **Supply Chain Optimization Simulator** built using **React**, **HTML**, **CSS**, and **Vanilla JavaScript**. The application teaches fundamental supply chain concepts through a guided, decision-based simulation where every choice impacts key business metrics in real time.

---

##  Overview

Supply Chain Builder is designed to help users understand how businesses design and optimize their supply chains. Instead of relying on theoretical explanations, the simulator provides an interactive learning experience where users make strategic decisions and immediately see their effects on cost, delivery performance, operational risk, customer satisfaction, and sustainability.

The application uses randomly generated company profiles, ensuring every playthrough presents a unique business scenario.

---

##  Features

-  Random company generation
-  Beginner-friendly educational explanations
-  Five-step supply chain building process
-  Live business metrics
-  Decision trade-off analysis
-  Final optimization dashboard
-  Overall Supply Chain Score
-  Strength & Weakness analysis
-  Risk identification
-  Practical business improvement recommendations
-  Replay with new randomized scenarios
-  Modern responsive dark-themed interface

---

##  Application Workflow

1. Start the simulation
2. Generate a random company profile
3. Choose a supplier strategy
4. Select a factory location
5. Decide on a warehouse strategy
6. Choose a transportation method
7. Select an inventory strategy
8. Monitor live business metrics after every decision
9. Review the final optimization dashboard
10. Replay with a new company scenario

---

##  Business Metrics

The simulator continuously updates the following metrics:

- Cost Efficiency
- Delivery Speed
- Risk Resilience
- Customer Satisfaction
- Sustainability

These metrics contribute to the final **Overall Supply Chain Score**.
