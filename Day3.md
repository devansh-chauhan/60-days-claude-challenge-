# Day 3 – Role-Based Prompting

## Objective

Learn how assigning different roles to Claude changes the quality, perspective, and focus of responses.

---

## Question Used

How can I build an AI-powered product while I am still a college student?

---

## Prompt 1: No Role

### Prompt

How can I build an AI-powered product while I am still a college student?

### Observation

The response was balanced and general. It covered multiple aspects but did not provide deep expertise from a specific perspective.

---

## Prompt 2: Founder Persona

### Prompt

Act as a successful startup founder who has built multiple AI companies.

How can I build an AI-powered product while I am still a college student?

Focus on:

* Finding a real problem
* Validating ideas
* Building an MVP
* Getting first users
* Avoiding common startup mistakes

Provide practical advice.

### Observation

The response focused heavily on customer problems, market validation, user acquisition, and business strategy. It encouraged thinking about solving real-world problems before building.

---

## Prompt 3: Developer Persona

### Prompt

Act as a senior AI engineer and software developer.

How can I build an AI-powered product while I am still a college student?

Focus on:

* Technical skills required
* AI tools and frameworks
* Project architecture
* Development roadmap
* Deployment and scaling

Provide practical technical guidance.

### Observation

The response focused on programming skills, AI frameworks, deployment, architecture, and technical implementation. It provided a more engineering-oriented roadmap.

---

## Comparison

| Aspect                  | No Role | Founder  | Developer |
| ----------------------- | ------- | -------- | --------- |
| Focus                   | General | Business | Technical |
| Market Advice           | Medium  | High     | Low       |
| Technical Detail        | Medium  | Low      | High      |
| User Acquisition        | Low     | High     | Low       |
| Implementation Guidance | Medium  | Medium   | High      |

---

## Key Learnings

1. AI responds differently depending on the assigned role.
2. Role-based prompting helps obtain expert-level perspectives.
3. The same question can generate completely different insights.
4. Founder personas focus on business outcomes.
5. Developer personas focus on implementation and technical execution.
6. Choosing the right role improves response quality and relevance.

---

## Claude Usage Counter

Installed and explored the Claude Usage Counter extension.

### Observations

* Displays usage statistics.
* Helps track Claude usage limits.
* Useful for managing conversations efficiently.

---

## Conclusion

Role-Based Prompting is a powerful technique that allows users to receive specialized advice from different perspectives. By defining a role clearly, the quality and usefulness of AI responses improve significantly.
