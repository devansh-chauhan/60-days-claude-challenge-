#  Task Compass — Learn How Work Flows Through Real Organizations

> Day 37 of the 60 Days of Claude Challenge

Task Compass is an interactive, single-file HTML learning simulation that teaches how work moves through organizations. Instead of memorizing job titles, users learn ownership, delegation, collaboration, escalation, and workflow thinking through realistic workplace scenarios.

##  Overview

Task Compass transforms organizational management concepts into a strategy-based simulation where players solve workplace challenges across multiple stages.

The application is designed to make organizational structures easy to understand for beginners while remaining engaging through interactive gameplay.

##  Features

-  Multiple workplace types (Tech Company, Startup, Hospital, School, Retail, Manufacturing, etc.)
-  Three progressive learning stages
-  Drag-and-drop interactions
-  Realistic workplace scenarios
-  Ownership and delegation exercises
-  Workflow routing simulation
-  Collaboration challenges
-  Organizational Thinking Dashboard
-  Progress tracking and completion celebration
-  Premium dark glassmorphism UI
-  Fully responsive design
-  Works completely offline

##  Learning Journey

### Stage 1 — Who Owns This?
Identify the most appropriate owner for real workplace tasks and understand why different roles contribute.

### Stage 2 — Task Routing
Build the correct workflow by routing tasks through the appropriate departments and teams.

### Stage 3 — Collaboration Challenge
Handle complex business situations by assigning multiple collaborating teams and understanding cross-functional communication.

### Final Reflection
Receive personalized insights on:

- Ownership Thinking
- Delegation
- Collaboration
- Workflow Understanding
