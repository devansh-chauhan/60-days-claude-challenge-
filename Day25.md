# Day 25 – AI Shark Tank Simulator

## Overview

For Day 25 of the 60 Days of Claude Challenge, I built and explored an AI Shark Tank Simulator that evaluates startup ideas through simulated investor questioning and scoring.

The application allows founders to pitch a startup idea, answer investor questions, receive dynamic feedback, and obtain investment recommendations.

## Features

* Startup Pitch Submission
* Four AI Investor Personas

  * Venture Capitalist
  * Founder
  * Customer
  * Angel Investor
* Interactive Question & Answer Flow
* Startup Scorecard
* Investment Decision Engine
* Suggested Valuation
* Funding Recommendations
* Leaderboard System
* PDF Pitch Report Download
* Share Results Feature
* Modern Shark Tank Style Interface

## Startup Evaluated

**PrepPilot**
An AI-powered placement preparation platform helping engineering students improve resumes, interview skills, and placement readiness.

## Results

* Overall Score: 82/100
* Investment Decision: INVEST
* Suggested Valuation: ₹4 Crore
* Recommended Funding: ₹50 Lakhs

## Key Learnings

* Investors evaluate markets more than features.
* Distribution is often more important than product quality.
* Strong business models require clear monetization paths.
* Validation and customer acquisition remain critical startup challenges.

## Outcome

Successfully explored startup evaluation, investor thinking, fundraising concepts, and business model analysis using AI-powered simulation.
