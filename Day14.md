# Day 14 — AI Job Red Flag Detector

## Challenge
Day 14 of the 60 Days of Claude Challenge focused on evaluating job opportunities critically instead of applying blindly.

The objective was to use Claude as an AI-powered Job Red Flag Detector to analyze a Job Description (JD) and Company Information, identify potential risks, and generate a detailed risk assessment report.

---

## Objective

Analyze a job opportunity and identify:

- Unrealistic requirements
- Toxic workplace indicators
- Remote work authenticity issues
- Hiring risks
- Company risks
- Positive signals
- Smart interview questions

---

## Input Provided

### Job Role
**Junior Full Stack Developer**

### Company Information
- Startup founded 8 months ago
- Team size: 12 employees
- No public funding information available
- Recent employee reviews mention long working hours
- No salary range disclosed
- Rapid hiring phase

---

## AI Analysis Generated

### Overall Risk Score
**88/100 — Critical Risk**

### Final Verdict
❌ **AVOID**

### Major Red Flags Identified

1. Junior role requiring 5+ years of experience
2. Excessively broad technology stack
3. Architecture ownership expected from a junior hire
4. Client leadership responsibilities
5. Mentoring expectations despite junior designation
6. 24/7 support requirement
7. Hidden relocation clause in a remote role
8. Work-hard-play-hard culture indicators
9. Missing salary transparency

---

## Risk Breakdown

| Category | Risk Level |
|-----------|-----------|
| Requirements | 9/10 |
| Culture | 9/10 |

| Hiring | 8/10 |
| Company | 7/10 |

---

## Positive Signals

- Opportunity to gain architecture exposure
- Small team can provide high visibility
- Potential AI/ML exposure
- Startup environment may offer faster learning

---

## Smart Interview Questions Generated

1. What would a typical week look like for a junior developer?
2. How is the 24/7 support expectation compensated?
3. Is relocation mandatory after probation?
4. What is the company's funding and runway status?
5. How does the team maintain work-life balance?

---

## Key Learnings

- Job titles can be misleading.
- "Junior" roles sometimes contain senior-level expectations.
- Remote jobs may include hidden relocation requirements.
- Salary transparency is an important trust signal.
- Company reviews provide valuable insights beyond the JD.
- AI can help identify risks that are easy to overlook manually.
